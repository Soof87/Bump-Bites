Daily nutrient targets
NZ supplements needed
💊 Folic acid 800 mcg/day — from before conception to 12 weeks
💊 Iodine 150 mcg/day — throughout pregnancy & breastfeeding
💊 Vitamin D — ask your LMC, especially in winter
Foods to avoid
🚫Raw or undercooked meat, chicken, and seafood
🚫Pre-made salads, deli meats, cold chicken, pâté (listeria risk)
🚫Unpasteurised dairy; brie, camembert, feta (unless cooked through)
🚫Shark, marlin, orange roughy, gemfish (high mercury)
🚫Liver and liver products (too much vitamin A)
🚫Alcohol — no safe level in pregnancy
⚠️Caffeine — limit to 200 mg/day (about 2 flat whites)
✅Safe NZ seafood: salmon, snapper, kahawai, tarakihi, mussels, cooked oysters

Always discuss your specific nutritional needs with your Lead Maternity Carer (LMC) or GP.
